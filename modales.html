CAN / COULD

Se usan para hablar de habilidad o posiblidad.

Estructura:

Afirmativa: Sujeto + Can (puedo) Could (podía) + Verbo en forma base + Complemento.

  • I can speak English.
  • She could swim when she was five.

Negativa: Sujeto + Can´t/cannot Could not/ couldn´t + Verbo en forma base + Complemento.

  • He couldn´t find his keys.
  • I can´t let you go.

Interrogativa: Can/Could + Sujeto + + Verbo en forma base + Complemento?

  • Could you swim when you were a child?
  • Can I go to the party?

MAY/MIGHT

Se usan para hablar de alguna posibilidad.

Estructura:

Afirmativa: Sujeto + May (puede) Might (podría) + Verbo en forma base + Complemento.

  • It may rain later.
  • I might stay home.

Negativa: Sujeto + May not / Might not + Verbo en forma base + Complemento.

  • You may not park here.
  • They might not like the movie.

Interrogativa: May/Might + Sujeto + Verbo en forma base + Complemento?

  • May I use your phone?
  • Might they be late?

MUST

Se usan para hablar sobre una obligación o necesidad.

Estructura:

Afirmativa: Sujeto + Must (deber/tener que) + Verbo en forma base + Complemento.

  • You must wear a seabelt.
  • He must be tired.

Negativa: Sujeto + Must not/mustn´t + Verbo en forma base + Complemento.

  • You must not smoke here.
  • Students mustn't cheat on the exam.

Interrogativa: Must + Sujeto + Verbo en forma base + Complemento?

  • Must she always be on time?
  • Must I do all the homework today?

SHOULD/OUGHT TO

Se usan para dar alguna recomendación o consejo.

Estructura:

Afirmativa: Sujeto + Should/Ought to (deberías) + Verbo en forma base + Complemento.

  • I should eat healthier.
  • You ought to rest.

Negativa: Sujeto + Ought to/Shouldn´t + Verbo en forma base + Complemento.

  • You ought not to lie.
  • He shouldn´t eat so much sugar.

Interrogativa: Should/Ought + Sujeto + to (solo para ought) + Verbo en forma base + Complemento?

  • Ought I to call her?
  • Should they wait outside?

WOULD

Se usan para preferencias, decisiones, acciones hipotéticas o peticiones formales u ofertas.

Estructura:

Afirmativa: Sujeto + Would + Verbo en forma base + Complemento.

  • I would travel if I had money.
  • She would like some tea.

Negativa: Sujeto + Wouldn´t + Verbo en forma base + Complemento.

  • I wouldn´t go there alone
  • He wouldn´t eat that.

Interrogativa: Would + Sujeto + Verbo en forma base + Complemento?

  • Would you like some coffee?
  • Would he come if we invited him?

BE SUPPOSED TO

Se usan en suposiciones.

Estructura:

Afirmativa: Sujeto + Am (I) Is (He,She,It) Are (They,We,You) + Supposed to (debe/se supone que) + Verbo en forma base + Complemento.

  • You´re supposed to finish your homework before class.
  • He´s supposed to be here.

Negativa: Sujeto + Am/Is/Are not + Supposed to + Verbo en forma base + Complemento.

  • You´re not supposed to be late.
  • We´re not supposed to eat in class.

Interrogativa: Am/Is/Are + Sujeto + supposed to + Verbo en forma base + Complemento?

  • Am I supposed to bring my ID?
  • Is he supposed to be here?
