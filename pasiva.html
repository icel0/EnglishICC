Se usan para:

  • Cuando no se sabe quién hizo la acción.
  • Cuando el sujeto no es importante.
  • Para sonar más formal y objetivo.
Estructura:

Objeto + Verbo "to be" (conjugado) + Verbo en pasado participio +

by + Sujeto.

Tiempo Voz activa Forma de "to be" Voz pasiva
Presente simple She cleans the room. am/is/are The room is cleaned by her.
Pasado Simple He wrote a letter. was/were A letter was written by him.
Futuro Simple They will finish the work. will be The work will be finished by them.
Presente Continuo She is painting the wall. am/is/are being The wall is being painted by her.
Pasado Continuo They were watching a movie. was/were being A movie was being watched by them.
Presente Perfecto He has broken the window. have/has been The window has been broken by him
Pasado Perfecto She had sent the email. had been The email had been sent by her.
Modales They must complete the project. modal + be The project must be completed by them.
