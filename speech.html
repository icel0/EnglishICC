Tiempo(directo) Estilo directo Tiempo(indirecto) Estilo indirecto
Presente simple She said, "I like tea". past simple She said she liked tea.
Presente Continuo He said, "I´m eating". past continuous He said he was eating
Presente Perfecto She said, "I have finished". past perfect She said she had finished.
Pasado Simple He said, "I saw the movie". past perfect He said he had seen the movie.
Pasado Continuo She said, "I was reading". past perfect continuous She said she had been reading.
Will He said, "I will help you". would He said he would help me.
Can She said, "I can swim". could She said she could swim.
May He said, "I may go". might He said he might go.
Must She said, "I must study". had to She said she had to study.
