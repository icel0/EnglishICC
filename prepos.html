TIEMPO

Preposición Uso/Significado Ejemplo
at Hora específica, festividades. at 5 o'clock, at midnight, at Christmas.
on Días y fechas específicas. on Monday, on July 4th, on my birthday.
in Meses, años, siglos, partes del día. in January, in 2003, in the morning.
for Duración. for two hours, for a week.
since Punto de inicio en el pasado. since 2010, since Monday.
by Antes de un tiempo específico. by tomorrow, by 5 pm.
from...to/ultil Desde...hasta. from 9 am to 5 pm, Monday until Friday.

LUGAR

Preposición Uso/Significado Ejemplo
at Punto específico o dirección. at the door, at the bus stop, at the corner.
on Sobre una superficie. on the table, on the wall, on the floor.
in Dentro de un espacio cerrado o área. in the room, in the box, in the city.
under Debajo. under the bed, under the table.
above Encima(sin tocar). above the fireplace.
below Debajo(sin tocar). bellow the surface.
next to/beside Al lado. next to the bank, beside the house.
between Entre dos cosas. between the two buildings.
behind Detrás. behind the school.
in front of Frente a in front of the car.
